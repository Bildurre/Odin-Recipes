Fruit And Yogurt Smoothie

Description

Absolutely delicious smoothie. Feel free to use any yogurt flavour you want and any kind of fresh fruit.

Best results with skimmed, sugar free yougurt for a healthy drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yougurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
